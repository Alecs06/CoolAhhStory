The wizard entered the damp, dark hall. He looked cautiously around for any 
possible dangers that might lurk in the far end of the hall, where his eyes

Suddenly the few torches that were fixed on the walls started flickering,
and one by one they were extinguished by the wind. The air started getting hotter 
and a shive ran down the spine of the wizard. Out of fear, the dyslexic wizard ducked down 
behind a sofa. After he quickly glanced around, he saw a door to his right
and sneakily entered through the door.
